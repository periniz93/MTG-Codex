Name Temur Gateway Menagerie
Transmogrify effects turn stray tokens into enormous monsters, letting the deck
play a draw-go game until it's time to drop Titans and Italis.

Deck
4 Transmogrify
4 Lukka, Coppercoat Outcast
4 Fable of the Mirror-Breaker
4 Big Score
4 Prismari Command
4 Unexpected Windfall
4 Make Disappear
4 Titan of Industry
4 Itali, Primal Conqueror
4 Stormcarved Coast
4 Rockfall Vale
4 Dreamroot Cascade
2 Cragcrown Pathway
2 Riverglide Pathway
2 Barkchannel Pathway
2 Sokenzan, Crucible of Defiance
2 Boseiju, Who Endures
2 Otawara, Soaring City

Notes
Fable provides the token bodies and filtering needed to set up Transmogrify,
while Make Disappear holds the fort until a Titan of Industry or Itali takes
over. Big Score and Windfall clear giant creatures from hand if drawn naturally,
replacing them with fresh interaction.
